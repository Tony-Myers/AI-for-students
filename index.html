Foundations: Mastering Prompt Engineering

Effective prompting is the key to unlocking AI's potential. It is the art and science of designing inputs to guide AI toward accurate, relevant, and useful outputs. These principles are based on empirical research and best practices from leading practitioners.

Download Prompt Templates

Get started quickly with our curated prompt templates for academic feedback and assessment.

Clarity & Specificity

Tell the AI exactly what you want, leaving no room for interpretation. Vague prompts lead to inconsistent outputs.

Instead of: "Produce a report based on this data"
Use: "List our five most popular products and write a one-paragraph description of each"

Context Provision

Provide all necessary background information to help the AI tailor its response to your specific needs.

Example: "I am a college senior with a 3.5 GPA and I need an essay outline on the French Revolution's impact"

Role Assignment

Assign a specific role or persona to influence tone, style, and domain expertise.

Example: "You are a patent lawyer. Explain the legal process for patenting an invention in simple terms"

Output Format

Clearly specify the desired structure for machine-readable or human-readable formats.

Example: "Return results in JSON format: {'key': 'value'}" or "Provide a bulleted list"
