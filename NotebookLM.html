Custom Quizzes & Practice Questions

Create tailored quizzes to test your understanding of the material. NotebookLM generates questions directly from your uploaded sources with answers and explanations.

How to Create Practice Quizzes:

Via Chat Interface:

  • "Create a 10-question multiple choice quiz on photosynthesis"
  • "Generate 5 essay questions about the French Revolution"
  • "Make 15 true/false questions covering all uploaded materials"
  • "Create application questions that test understanding, not just recall"

Specify Difficulty Levels:

  • "Create easy recall questions for initial learning"
  • "Generate medium difficulty application questions"
  • "Make challenging analysis questions for exam prep"
  • "Mix of 40% easy, 40% medium, 20% hard questions"

Progressive Study Strategy: Start with basic recall questions in week 1, move to application questions in week 2, then analysis questions in week 3 leading up to exams.

Flashcard Generation

Generate flashcards for active recall practice. NotebookLM can create term-definition pairs, question-answer cards, or concept explanation cards from your materials.

Creating Effective Flashcards:

Example Prompts:

  • "Create 20 flashcards covering all key terms from my psychology notes"
  • "Make flashcards for mathematical formulas with example problems"
  • "Generate concept flashcards explaining 'how' and 'why', not just definitions"
  • "Create two-way flashcards: term to definition AND definition to term"

Different Flashcard Types:

  • Basic: Term on front, definition on back
  • Concept: Question on front, explanation on back
  • Application: Scenario on front, solution on back
  • Formula: Formula name on front, formula and example on back

Active Recall Strategy:

Review new cards daily, then every 3 days, then weekly. This spaced repetition ensures long-term retention.

Export & Integration:

Copy flashcards to Anki or Quizlet for automatic scheduling, mobile access, and tracking your progress over time.
