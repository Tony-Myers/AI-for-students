University AI Assessment Framework

Understanding your assignment's AI policy is crucial for academic success. Most universities now use a clear framework to specify what AI use is allowed for each assignment. Always check your assignment brief for the specific level.

Each assignment will specify which level applies. When in doubt, ask your lecturer before using any AI tools.

Level 1: No AI

üö´ Prohibited

The assessment is completed entirely without AI assistance. This level ensures that students rely solely on their own knowledge, understanding, and skills.

Requirement:

AI must not be used at any point during the assessment.

Examples:

Closed-book exams, in-class tests, traditional essays requiring original thought

Level 2: AI-Assisted Idea Generation & Structuring

üí≠ Planning Only

AI can be used for brainstorming, creating structures, and generating ideas for improving work.

Requirement:

No AI content is allowed in the final submission.

Examples:

Using AI to brainstorm essay topics, create outlines, or generate research questions

Level 3: AI-Assisted Editing

‚úèÔ∏è Editing Only

AI can be used to make improvements to the clarity or quality of student-created work, but no new content can be created using AI.

Requirement:

Your original work with no AI content must be provided in an appendix.

Examples:

Grammar checking, improving sentence clarity, formatting assistance

Level 4: AI Task Completion, Human Evaluation

ü§ñ AI + Analysis

AI is used to complete certain elements of the task, with students providing discussion or commentary on the AI-generated content. This requires critical engagement with AI-generated content.

Requirement:

You will use AI to complete specified tasks in your assessment. Any AI-created content must be cited.

Examples:

AI generates data analysis, you interpret and critique the results

Level 5: Collaborating with AI

ü§ù Partnership

AI should be used as a 'co-pilot' to meet the requirements of the assessment, allowing for a collaborative approach with AI and enhancing creativity.

Requirement:

You may use AI throughout your assessment to support your own work and do not have to specify which content is AI-generated.

Examples:

Creative projects, innovative problem-solving, collaborative research

Level 6: Must Use AI

‚úÖ Required

AI usage is mandatory, aiming to build confidence and proficiency with AI tools.

Requirement:

You must use AI throughout this assessment and demonstrate how you used and refined outputs.

Examples:

AI literacy courses, technology integration projects, future-focused assignments
